Kids & Teens Tuition

Students between the ages of 5 and 16 -- from Kindergarten to Year 12 -- are provided tutoring in English, Math, and Science, as well as exam preparation in IELTS, TOEFL, IGCSE, ACT, and SAT.

Qualiﬁed tutors engage students in interactive exercises in:

  • English grammar, reading, spelling, comprehension, vocabulary, phonemic awareness, and written expression
  • Math concepts from basics to advanced
  • Physics, Chemistry, and Biology

Students are motivated creatively with the aim to boost their conﬁdence levels and make learning fun and engaging.

An assessment will identify each student’s learning needs and facilitate the development of a personalized learning program. Parents are provided with regular reports and updates to track their child’s progress.
